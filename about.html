Meet Ra

Ra Harris is a highly skilled and well-connected Realtor licensed with Samson Properties. Having lived all over the DC, Maryland, and Virginia Metropolitan areas, Ra is a local real estate market expert.

Ra's client-centered approach ensures a joyful experience from end to end. She is transparent, has integrity, and is committed to facilitating a hassle-free process.

Demystifying real estate is her priority when meeting with new clients. Her mission is to help others see their vision through to reality and succeed at their real estate goals!

Transparency

Clear communication about the steps taken to market and negotiate property, ensures that you feel secure in every phase of the decision-making process

Integrity

Remarkable accountability, honesty and ethical conduct to protect your best interest in today's competitive real estate market.

Commitment

Unwavering dedication and exemplary service devoted to help you swiftly navigate the complexities of the entire property transaction process.

Services

Whether buying, selling, renting, or investing, schedule a meeting with Ra to explore all the options that will lead to a successful transaction. See the list of real estate services Ra provides below.

Buyers Agent

Are you looking to buy? First-time homebuyer? Ready to build your dream home or find a headquarters for your start-up? Ra develops an understanding of her client's real estate and financial needs to find the perfect property.

Listing Agent for Sellers

Ra facilitates a fluid transition for owners looking to sell a property. Equipped with a diligent media team, Ra guarantees the most skillful marketing of your home or business and closing that won't leave her client settling for less.

Tenant Agent for Renting

Searching for properties to lease on your own can become tedious and, more times than not, leads to an endless search. Leave the grunt work to Ra and have her represent you as a tenant.

Investing

Are you an investor or want to learn more about income-producing properties, land deals, building equity over time, fix-n-flips, renovations, and more? Set up a consultation with Ra today.
